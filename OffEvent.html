Offline Events

CodeVein Offline Meeting

Description of the event:

CodeVein offline meeting is an annual activity for all CodeVein players. It is a good chance for the players to know each others from online to offline. Players have chances to share their experiences or feelings during the meeting. Besides, famous broadcasters are invited to give talks. CodeVein peripheral products such as models, stationery and so on are sold.

Time:

4th June 2021

Location:

Malaysia

CodeVein SpeedRun

Description of the event:

CodeVein SpeedRun is an annual competition for all CodeVein players. This is a fair competition for players to enter a same mission, the fastest player who complete the mission is the champion. There are various map that can be chosen by the players, players can apply for any one or more of the mission. Abundant prizes are given to the first three players of each map.

Time:

14th June 2021

Location:

Malaysia
